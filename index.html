How to Use This Index

Stress Index

Measures current market pressure. A higher value suggests stronger fear, risk aversion, and market stress.

Opportunity Index

Measures whether the market is becoming attractive for gradual accumulation. A higher value suggests better buy-on-weakness conditions.

Suggested Interpretation

  • 0–25: Calm / low opportunity
  • 25–50: Neutral / monitor
  • 50–75: Watchlist / consider scaling in
  • 75–100: High opportunity / accumulate carefully
